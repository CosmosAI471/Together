Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Important to Us

Your personal data is securely stored in Firebase. We do not sell your information, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Security Risks

Users can create fake accounts. Be cautious when interacting with strangers. We do not verify user identities.

Database Limitations

If you experience issues sending messages or adding contacts, it may be due to our Firestore database being full. We regularly clear space, but delays may occur.
